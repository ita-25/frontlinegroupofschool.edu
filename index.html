Academic Excellence is a Necessity

Frontline Group of Schools, led by Proprietor Deacon Sunny Osuhon (Chairman, NAPPS Ika South), is one of the largest and most respected schools in Agbor, Delta State. We accommodate over 640 pupils and students across Nursery, Primary, and Secondary levels, built on a foundation of 100% Academic Pass Rates, strong Moral Training, and Skill Development.

Why Choose Frontline?

Academic Excellence

Achieved 100% pass rate in all external examinations (WAEC, NECO, BECE) in 2025. We uphold rigorous standards for academic success.

Skills Acquisition

Practical skill development programs like perfume and liquid soap making to equip students with entrepreneurship skills for life after school.

Character Formation

Committed to strong moral values, discipline, and producing God-fearing graduates who are ready to lead and serve their communities.
