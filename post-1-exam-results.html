Unprecedented Success: Frontline Achieves 100% Pass Rate in All External Exams!

Published: October 16, 2025 | By: Academic News Team | Academics, Recognition

[span_0](start_span)Frontline Group of Schools, Agbor, has marked a historic milestone, achieving a **perfect 100% pass rate** across all major external examinations in 2025. This stellar performance spanned the **WAEC, NECO, BECE (JSS3), and Primary 6 Cognitive exams**[span_0](end_span).

[span_1](start_span)This extraordinary result is a direct reflection of the dedication and high standards upheld by the school, which accommodates over 640 pupils and students across Nursery, Primary, and Secondary levels[span_1](end_span). [span_2](start_span)The school, led by Proprietor Deacon Sunny Osuhon and Head Teacher Mrs. Marian Osuhon, is built on a foundation of academic excellence, moral training, and skill development[span_2](end_span).
